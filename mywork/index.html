Integrated Calendar App

Show your customers your availability with the Integrated Calendar. In just a few clicks the integrated calendar will sync with all your reservations on Airbnb, VRBO, Booking.com, or any other 3rd party resource that provides an ICS file. Currently available on Wix, and coming soon to Wordpress.

To add the Integrated Calendar to your website on Wix download it from the Wix Marketplace. If you'd like to add the app to your website not hosted on Wix or Wordpress contact us for installation and pricing information.
